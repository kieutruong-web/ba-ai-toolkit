# Business Requirements Document (BRD)

## 1. Introduction

This document outlines the high-level business requirements for the E-commerce Web Application (MVP).

The purpose is to enable online purchasing and validate product-market fit.

---

## 2. Business Goals

- Increase sales by enabling online transactions
- Reduce dependency on physical stores
- Improve customer accessibility
- Enable scalable digital operations

---

## 3. Business Requirements

### BR-01: User Account Management
The system shall allow customers to create and manage personal accounts.

### BR-02: Product Browsing
The system shall allow customers to browse available products.

### BR-03: Shopping Cart
The system shall allow customers to add and manage items in a shopping cart.

### BR-04: Order Placement
The system shall allow customers to place orders successfully.

### BR-05: Order Confirmation
The system shall notify customers after successful order placement.
